Data Analyst

A Data Analyst is a professional who transforms raw data into meaningful insights that help organizations make better decisions. Using tools like Excel, SQL, Python, and visualization software, they collect, clean, and analyze data to identify trends, solve problems, and support strategic planning. Whether in finance, marketing, healthcare, or technology, Data Analysts play a key role in turning numbers into actionable recommendations that drive business success.
