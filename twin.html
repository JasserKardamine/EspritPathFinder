Everything You Need to Know

The TWIN (Technologies Web et Images Numériques) option orients the training of future engineers toward careers in web and internet technologies.

Its objective is to strengthen student competencies in various application areas, such as web development languages, frameworks, user experience (UX), search engine optimization (SEO), as well as data analysis techniques including Web Analytics, Data Mining, and Big Data.
